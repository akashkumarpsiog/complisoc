# Complisoc

AI-Powered Automated Security Compliance Testing Platform

Version: 1.0

---

# Executive Summary

Complisoc is an AI-powered platform that transforms security findings into compliance intelligence.

The platform continuously scans target environments, normalizes findings, maps findings to compliance controls, verifies mappings, generates reports, and produces audit-ready evidence.

The goal is to provide continuous compliance assurance for frameworks such as SOC2 and ISO27001 while reducing manual compliance effort.

Semester 1 focuses on demonstrating an end-to-end compliance intelligence pipeline rather than replacing auditors or certification processes.

---

# Problem Statement

Organizations struggle with:

* Point-in-time compliance validation
* Infrastructure drift
* Fragmented audit evidence
* Manual compliance workflows
* Limited governance visibility

Traditional audits provide snapshots rather than continuous assurance.

---

# Product Vision

Transform technical security findings into explainable compliance intelligence.

Complisoc bridges the gap between:

* Security Operations
* Engineering Teams
* Compliance Teams
* Auditors
* Leadership

---

# Product Goals

* Continuously identify security findings
* Normalize findings from multiple scanners
* Map findings to compliance controls
* Verify mappings using AI
* Generate governance reports
* Produce audit-ready evidence
* Maintain historical compliance visibility

---

# Supported Frameworks

* SOC2
* ISO27001:2022

Controls are discovered through findings and mapped dynamically.

Semester 1 success criteria require 15+ validated control mappings across supported frameworks.
